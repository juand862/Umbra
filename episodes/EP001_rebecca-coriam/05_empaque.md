## DESCRIPCIÓN

```
Rebecca Coriam was 24 years old when she disappeared from a Disney cruise ship in March 2011. 
She was filmed crying on an internal phone at 5:45 a.m. — wearing clothes that weren't hers. 
Hours later, she was gone. The Bahamas police closed the case in a single day as an accidental 
death: a freak wave from Deck 5. But the one piece of evidence placing her on that deck belonged 
to someone else and didn't even fit her. And the CCTV footage handed to her family had its 
location data removed. This episode examines what the official investigation missed — and why 
the flag-of-convenience system means families like the Coriams are left to fund the real 
inquiry themselves.

Keywords: Rebecca Coriam, Disney Wonder disappearance, cruise ship missing persons, flag of 
convenience jurisdiction, CCTV evidence manipulation, Royal Bahamas Police Force investigation, 
unsolved deaths at sea, Roy Ramm Scotland Yard.
```
